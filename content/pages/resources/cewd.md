The **Chinese Emotion Word Database (CEWD)** features a collection of **598
emotion words** identified through an extensive literature review on emotion
words and facial expressions. From this set, **99 emotion words** were selected
based on high emotional typicality and distinctiveness from other words.

For each of the 99 emotion words, the database provides:

- Participants' ratings of valence and arousal.
- The proportion of participants who reported that they typically express the
  emotion through seven channels: *voice, facial expression, body movement,
  verbal language, touch, art, and solely internal experience.*
- The references from which these emotion words were identified.

## Access

To access the CEWD, please complete and print the Database Usage Agreement.
After signing it by hand, send a scanned PDF copy of the agreement to
[x.fang@zju.edu.cn](mailto:x.fang@zju.edu.cn).
