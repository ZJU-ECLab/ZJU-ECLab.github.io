This summer, the Emotion and Culture Lab is excited to welcome a group of
international exchange students from **Canada, Slovenia and Norway** as part of
the **GRIPS 2025 (Global Research and Innovation Program for Students)**
initiative.

The participants are:

- **Minqian (Charlie) Shen** — Queen's University, Canada
- **Nika Pušlar** — University of Ljubljana, Slovenia
- **Rosalie Lai** — Norwegian School of Economics, Norway
- **Talia Peters** — University of Alberta, Canada

We are thrilled to have them join us in **Hangzhou** and become part of our
vibrant research community. Over the coming weeks, they will engage in hands-on
research, cultural exchange, and collaborative learning alongside our faculty and
students.

We look forward to a summer of exploration, connection, and shared discovery.
Welcome to the lab—and to Zhejiang University!
